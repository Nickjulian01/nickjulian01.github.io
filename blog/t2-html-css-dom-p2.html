What is grid based design

grid based design is a structure used to help people easily follow the information \ on the page. its used in newspapers and on most webpages. it lays out the information from left to right and usually starts with a title followed by a heading then sometimes an image and last but not least the content/ paragraph

What's all the hype about a responsive webpage?

responsive webpages are very neccesary in this day and age as people are using all sorts of different sized screens. from phones to tablets to large p.c monitors. responsiveness allows the page to adapt to new sizes while still portraying the same information.
